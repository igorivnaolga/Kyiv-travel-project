Why Kyiv?

Kyiv, the city of courage

As you probably know now is unsafe to travel to Ukraine because of war. But I believe that very soon Ukraine will win and become open for travelers. At the moment Kyiv is an epicentr of struggle for freedom and democracy. Remarkable story of Ukrainian courage and resilience is unfolding that has to be seen to be truly appreciated. Real value of a visit to Kyiv comes from observing European history taking shape in real time. So get ready to visit Kyiv in near future to really feel yourself as a part of history.
